About Us

Explore PH Tours is a travel and tours company with branches in Manila, Cebu, and Davao. We specialize in offering exciting travel packages, promos, and services to customers exploring the Philippines. Explore PH Tours prides itself on being the gateway to unforgettable adventures across the Philippines. Whether travelers are seeking pristine beaches, bustling cityscapes, or serene mountain escapes, our company curates experiences tailored to every preference. With branches strategically located in Manila, Cebu, and Davao, Explore PH Tours ensures easy access to the most sought-after destinations across Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao. Our services include guided city tours, island-hopping adventures, mountain trekking packages, and customized travel itineraries that allow customers to explore the unique beauty of the Philippines in comfort and style. We also offer hotel and transport reservations, making trip planning seamless and hassle-free. We are committed to providing both locals and international tourists with the best value through attractive promotions and packages. From discounts on advance bookings to exclusive group deals, Explore PH Tours ensures every customer gets more from their travel budget. Special packages like the Historic Intramuros Tour in Manila, Whale Shark Watching in Cebu, and Mt. Apo Climbing in Davao highlights the rich cultural and natural heritage of the country. Backed by a team of professional guides and friendly staff, Explore PH Tours goes the extra mile to make every journey memorable. Our motto, "Your Gateway to the Philippines," reflects our passion for helping customers create lifelong memories through travel.
